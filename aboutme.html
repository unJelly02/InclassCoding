My UX Skills

User Research

During research my main goal is to focus on SWOT analysis and find ways to improve the design to make it more User friendly.

User Empathy

For user empathy ,I usually test with a wide range of users to find out their opinion on the design and make changes accordingly.

Qualitative Interviews

During qualitative interviews with the users I tend to have less questions but tend to have them mostly open ended so they would give more insights without me asking.
